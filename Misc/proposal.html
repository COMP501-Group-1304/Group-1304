    Chosen Topic: Gaming in Academia


    Many technologies used and developed for the gaming experience can be used in various fields of research. Academic research is an integral parttoimproving our society and using technology developed and used in gaming is a way to be able to get access to new tech which could potentially improve life quality and increase our understanding of the world around us. Gaming has been used to contribute to large-scale scientific research such as genome sequencing and protein folding. VR technologies such as motion tracking, haptic feedback, and realistic physics engines advance our gaming and help our understanding of physics systems and modelling.

    The same technology being developed for gaming is being developed for simulations, such as Microsoft Flight Simulator, which pilots use for training or driving simulations for racecar drivers. 

    We can see digital simulations have a real-life impact on society and have an important role in contributing to our technological advancements and how they impact our day to day lives.
